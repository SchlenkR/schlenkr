General information on data processing

This website processes personal data only to the extent necessary for providing a functional website and its contents. Processing happens on the legal basis of Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interests).

Server log data

When you visit this site, the hosting provider automatically collects technical data (IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, time of request). This data is stored temporarily and is not merged with other data sources.

External resources

This site loads fonts and JavaScript libraries from third-party CDNs: Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com) for web fonts, and jsdelivr.net for GSAP, ScrollTrigger, DrawSVGPlugin, MorphSVGPlugin, and Lenis. When these resources are loaded, your IP address is transmitted to the respective provider. No personal data beyond this technical request is collected by the site operator.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies. Some variants may use local storage (for example to remember a view-mode toggle). Local storage never leaves your browser.

Contact by email

If you contact us by email, your request including all personal data arising from it (name, email address, enquiry) is stored and processed for the purpose of handling your request. This data is not passed on without your consent.

Your rights

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Germany, the state data-protection commissioner of your state of residence.
