How the AI Adoption Review works.

01 / Engagement
Step 01 · Listen

Diagnose the real question.

A working session with your product and engineering leads. What are you actually trying to change — revenue, throughput, cost, experience? No slide deck, no vendor pitch.

Step 02 · Read

Walk the codebase and data.

I read what you have. Architecture, data shape, team shape. I identify the places where AI genuinely fits and the places where a simpler heuristic would ship first.

Step 03 · Shortlist

Three to five moves, ranked.

A defensible shortlist of AI moves scoped to your stack and your people. Each with effort estimate, risk, and a concrete success check. No "platform" recommendation.

Step 04 · Hand over

A document your team owns.

A written argument — what's worth doing, what to skip, what to revisit in six months — and a working conversation with your team so the reasoning stays in-house after I'm gone.

Less than hiring a senior full-time. Scoped explicitly — no retainer trap, no seat-based staffing arithmetic. You know the boundary before we start.

An agency builds more capacity. I make your existing team capable. Different job. The deliverable is a document and a defensible decision, not a delivery team.

Yes. DACH and remote EU, both supported. Working sessions over video, code walkthroughs in your repo, written output you can forward in the next board meeting. Onsite possible for team-facing days.
